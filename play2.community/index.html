How to receive Play2World Tokens

We aim to be the simplest new token event of 2022

In the Token Generation Event, the Foundation will create enough Play2World new tokens to match the number of Play2World reward points ever produced eg Play2HelpWorld reward points.

For each of the old reward points in your Play2 accounts eg Play2Help reward points, you will be able to claim 1 new Play2World token without losing your old reward point balance eg Play2Help rewards balance.

Step 1

Download the Play2World App

The app is available in these countries. We are constantly adding new countries – sign up with your email address below to get updates on our global expansion.

Step 2

create or login to your Play2 account

Now that you have successfully downloaded the app, create or login to your Play2coin account.

Step 3

Play2World Wallet

Go to Marketplace, and then Blockchain Token Wallet, where you can create your Play2World Wallet.

Step 4

Get playing

Start playing to earn Play2World tokens on the apps. For every Play2 reward point in your account that you have been rewarded for play, you will received an equal amount of new Play2World tokens at the Token Generation Event, without losing any of your old Play2 reward points.

Step 5

Stay in touch for updates

The Play2World is moving fast. We don’t want anyone to miss out. Follow us on our social channels, or sign up with your email address.
